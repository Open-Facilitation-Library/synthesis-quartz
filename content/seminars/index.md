## About the Series

These seminars bring together practitioners, researchers, and technologists to explore how AI can support (not replace) human facilitation in democratic processes.

**Hosts**: Artem Zhiganov, Metagov Interoperable Deliberative Tools Programme

**Format**: ~60-70 minute sessions with presentation and Q&A
