# Alice Siu: Stanford Online Deliberation Platform

**Date**: November 10, 2025
**Speaker**: Alice Siu, Stanford University
**Duration**: ~50 minutes

---

## Overview

Alice Siu presented the Stanford Online Deliberation Platform (ODP), which has expanded to over 40 countries and operates in more than 35 languages.

## Platform Capabilities

### Global Reach
- 40+ countries
- 35+ languages
- Replicates in-person deliberative polling online

### AI-Assisted Features

The platform uses AI to replicate human moderator functions:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Agenda Management** | Keeps discussions on track |
| **Speaking Queue Control** | Ensures equitable participation |
| **Offensive Language Detection** | Maintains respectful discourse |

## Research Findings

### Online vs. In-Person Deliberation

Studies demonstrate:
- Similar opinion changes in online vs. in-person formats
- Comparable trust-building effects
- Cost and accessibility advantages online

### Real-World Impact

Significant outcomes including:
- Constitutional amendments in Mongolia
- Policy influence in multiple democracies
- Large-scale citizen engagement

## Deliberation on Demand

New interface in development:
- Users can create and manage their own AI-assisted deliberations
- Customizable briefing materials
- Expected release in 3-6 months

## User Experience Focus

Platform improvements through:
- Participant feedback integration
- Flexible facilitation options
- Balance between automation and participant empowerment

## Question Synthesis

Exploring automation of:
- Synthesizing participant questions
- Balancing AI assistance with participant control
- Maintaining participant agency in the process

## Strategic Vision

Core focus areas:
- **Equitable deliberation** - Access for all
- **Innovation with humanity** - Technology serving human needs
- **Scalability** - Reaching more people affordably
